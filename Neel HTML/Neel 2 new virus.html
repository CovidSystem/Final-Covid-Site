Phases 1 and 2: A New Virus

Phase 1 begins with a virus present in an animal. At this point it can not spread to humans and is not a threat to humans.

Phase 2 happens when the virus is shown to be able to spread to humans. At this point it is considered to be a risk of leading to a pandemic. It is important to note that it is rare for a virus to jump between species and to do so it must be able replicate itself in the new host.
