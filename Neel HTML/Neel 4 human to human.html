Phase 4: Human to Human Transmission

Phase 4 occurs when there is enough human-to-human infection happening that it leads to community outbreaks. This is cause for concern. It's also worth noting however that in order to get here that a virus must be able to replecate itself and infect other hosts. This is very rare.
