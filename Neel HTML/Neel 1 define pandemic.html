What is a Pandemic?

The World Health Organization defines a pandemic as "worldwide spread of a new disease." When a new disease emerges it can spread exponentially due to peoples lack of immunity. WHO announces the emergence of a new pandemic and breaks donw the growth of a pandemic into 6 phases which will be described in the following pages.
